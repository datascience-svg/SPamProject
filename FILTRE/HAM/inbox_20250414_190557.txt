Dear Prospective Student,

As part of the I School's continued commitment to shape the future of data science by promoting its efforts to serve the public good, the I School offers the Jack Larson Data for Good Fellowship <https://ischoolonline.berkeley.edu/admissions/tuition-financial-aid/fellowships/>. This fellowship is designed to support MIDS students in their pursuit of a career that will allow them to improve human life and benefit society as a whole.

One $8,500 fellowship will be awarded to one MIDS student per cohort over the next two years. Eligible applicants will have demonstrated how they intend to use the skills they will acquire in the MIDS program to drive social change.
